    Weingut Joebstl Schilcher “Ried Krass”

    still wine » rosé wine

    Schilcher is the traditional style of rosé of a small area in the western part of Styria in Austria. Made from the local grape, Blauer Wildbacher, these wines are famously refreshing when paired with the rich local pork and fish dishes, as Schilcher is one of the highest-acid, dry, vinifera wines in the world. Joebstl takes grapes from Ried Krass, at the very top of the Schilcher hill, to make an uncommonly intricate rose with herbal and floral notes and a zippy, strawberry-tinged finish.
